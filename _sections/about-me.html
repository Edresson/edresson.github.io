I'm a 2st year Ph.D student in computer science at the Institute of Mathematics and Computer Sciences (ICMC), University of São Paulo (USP).

Recently, I'm a member of NILC (Interinstitutional Center for Computational Linguistics), a renowned NLP Research group in Brazil and I participate in R&D projects in Speech at CEIA (Center of Excellence in Artificial Intelligence)

I took my bachelor's degree in Computer Science at the Federal University of Technology – Paraná. I started to learn about ML and NLP for my final project, under the supervision of Arnaldo Candido Junior . As a result, I created and made available a dataset for speech synthesis in Brazilian Portuguese, and developed a Speech synthesizer (TTS) for the Brazilian Portuguese publicly available.
